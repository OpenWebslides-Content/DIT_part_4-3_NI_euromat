Mechanical neutron velocity selector

Velocity selector

  • Moderate Energy-resolution (Δλ/λ≈15%)
  • High flux
  • Large FOV (~10x10cm)
  • Some wavelength shift accross FOV

Peetermans, S.; Grazzi, F.; Salvemini, F. & Lehmann, E. Spectral characterization of a velocity selector type monochromator for energy-selective neutron imaging. Physics Procedia, 2013, 43, 121-127

Double crystal monochromator

Double crystal monochromator

  • (pyrolithic graphite)
  • Better energy resolution (Δλ/λ≈2%-5%)
  • Lower flux
  • Smaller FOV
  • Higher order contamination

TESI: bandwidth filter

TESI

  • Crystal (PG) based bandwidth filter
  • Combined with VS
  • Δλ/λ≈2%-5%
  • No higher order contamination

Peetermans, S.; Josic, L.; Swygenhoven, H. V. & Lehmann, E. H. A new monochromator for energy-selective imaging, presentation, Neuwave-4, 2011
